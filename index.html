Precast sandwich panels are composite wall systems consisting of three main layers:
1. Inner Concrete Layer (Wythe): Structural concrete layer providing internal support.
2. Insulation Core: Acts as a thermal barrier.
3. Outer Concrete Layer (Wythe): External protective and structural layer.
In our panels we also add steel reinforcement mesh to additionally strengthen the panels and vapor barer. With our innovative design we achieve R-Value over 18 times higher than standard brick and XPS wall.

Precast sandwich panels offer a combination of strength, fire and sound protection, thermal efficiency, and rapid construction, making them a popular choice in modern construction projects. They are suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings where efficiency and durability are required.
All elements of our build system are used for many years in commercial building , our innovation is connecting this into one system focused on efficiency.
